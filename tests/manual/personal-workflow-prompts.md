# Manual Validation Prompts

Use these prompts to test whether the skill produces a sharp investor-oriented
AI news digest.

## Prompts

1. Summarize the most important China and overseas AI news in the last 48 hours and rank it by investment relevance.
2. Help me build a daily AI news digest for a primary-market investor focused on startups and platform shifts.
3. These are 10 AI headlines. Sort them into important, watch, and noise, then explain why.
4. Compare the most important AI developments in China versus the US this week from an investor perspective.
5. Which recent AI news items are most likely to affect startup formation, fundraising, or competitive moats?

## What to Check

- Does the ranking reflect investment importance rather than media volume?
- Does the output separate fact from interpretation?
- Does it highlight China versus overseas implications clearly?
- Does it stay concise enough for fast daily use?
