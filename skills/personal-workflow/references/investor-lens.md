# Investor Lens

Use this file to tune the digest toward the user's actual investment style.

## Default Focus

- China and cross-border AI developments
- primary-market implications instead of public-market price action
- startup formation, funding momentum, and moat shifts
- model, application, infrastructure, chip, and policy developments

## Rank Higher By Default

- changes in compute supply or inference economics
- policy shifts affecting model deployment or data access
- major model releases that open or close startup opportunities
- large enterprise adoption signals tied to real budget movement
- financing, M&A, or shutdowns that reset category expectations

## Rank Lower By Default

- superficial feature launches
- weakly sourced rumors
- generic conference commentary
- partnership announcements without distribution or revenue implications
- trend pieces with no new fact pattern

## Personalization Hooks

Update this file over time with:

- preferred sectors
- priority companies to watch
- recurring false positives
- signals that historically mattered most in your work
