# Workflow Patterns

## High-Signal News Types

Prioritize items such as:

- major model releases or capability jumps
- AI chip, cloud, or inference cost shifts
- material regulatory or policy changes
- large platform distribution changes
- strategic fundraising, M&A, or shutdowns that change market structure
- enterprise adoption signals with clear budget movement

## Lower-Signal News Types

Be cautious with:

- generic partnership announcements
- vanity metrics without context
- small feature launches
- rumor-heavy coverage
- repetitive commentary that adds no new fact pattern

## Ranking Heuristics

### Rank Higher When

- the news changes company formation or funding opportunities
- the news affects competitive moats or value capture
- the news shifts supply chain power in chips, cloud, or data
- the news matters specifically for China market access or global expansion
- the signal is likely to persist beyond a few days

### Rank Lower When

- the item is mostly publicity
- the facts are thin
- there is no credible implication for startup or investor behavior
- the development is already fully priced into market expectations

## Interpretation Rules

- Separate reported facts from your inference.
- Explain whether the signal helps incumbents, startups, or both.
- Note whether the effect is near-term, medium-term, or structural.
- If relevance differs between China and overseas, say so explicitly.

## Good Output Shapes

- top 5 ranked digest
- important / watch / ignore
- what happened / why it matters / investment view
